10,000 Roses Cafe in Cordova

Have you been to the very famous destination among local tourists in Cebu? The 10000 Roses in Cordova, Cebu has now become one of the most famous tourist attractions in Cebu today. Most locals flocked their way to visit the popular site with ten thousand roses. The place is not just a garden of roses but also a cafe which is right next to Lantaw Floating Restaurant, a popular restaurant in the municipality of Cordova

the artificial flower was so much beautiful especially at night. you can take photo and video of it to enjoy the scene and view

my favorate part about exploring 10K roses is the beautiful unexpected sunset.

In our world full of episodes, how we deal with the different hindrances we are facing matters a lot. Sometimes, we are stuck to our daily routines and commitment, and the internet around us eventually gets most of our time.

Bantayan Bay at Bantayan, Cordova, is a budget friendly outing place for friends and family. You can have your stay at the Sandbar or along the mangroves for a shade.

Whats more is that you can enjoy a instagrammable sunset painted sky when the sun is setting.
